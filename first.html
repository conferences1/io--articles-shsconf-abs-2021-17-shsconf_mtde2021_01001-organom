Annotation.
Currently, the Russian Federation has the world's largest reserves of fresh water resources, which are an integral part of the national wealth. However, the modern mechanism of exploitation of water resources benefits does not meet the requirements of sustainable development of the regions of the Russian Federation, as it is focused on achieving the highest possible economic results and is based on ensuring an ever-increasing consumption of water resources, which determines the extensive format of their economic development and unprecedented exploitation of the resource and raw material potential of Russian water bodies, which actualizes the need to study directions for improving the efficiency of the regional water management complex.
